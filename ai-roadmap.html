AI Roadmap

Your pathway to mastering Artificial Intelligence in 2024

Step 1: Basics of AI

Understand what AI is, its types (narrow, general, superintelligence), and its applications.

Step 2: Machine Learning

Learn about supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms and their use cases.

Step 3: Deep Learning

Master neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, and deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch.

Step 4: Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Understand how AI can process and generate human language.

Step 5: Computer Vision

Learn about image recognition, object detection, and computer vision frameworks.

Step 6: Reinforcement Learning

Explore Q-learning, deep Q-networks (DQNs), and how machines learn through trial and error.

Step 7: AI in Real-World Applications

Apply AI to solve real-world problems in areas like healthcare, finance, and robotics.
