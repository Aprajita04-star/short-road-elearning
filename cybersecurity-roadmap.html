Cybersecurity Roadmap

Your pathway to mastering Cybersecurity in 2024

Step 1: Basics of Networking

Understand the fundamentals of computer networks, IP addressing, and protocols.

Step 2: Introduction to Security

Learn about security principles like confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA).

Step 3: Cryptography

Learn how encryption and decryption work, along with hash functions and digital signatures.

Step 4: Ethical Hacking

Learn about penetration testing, ethical hacking tools, and practices.

Step 5: Network Defense

Explore firewalls, intrusion detection systems (IDS), and VPNs.

Step 6: Incident Response & Forensics

Learn how to handle cyber attacks, incident response, and digital forensics techniques.

Step 7: Security Automation & Tools

Explore security automation, SIEM tools, and threat intelligence platforms.
